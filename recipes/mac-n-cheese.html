Mac N' Cheese

Our recipe for Easy Creamy Mac N' Cheese has only 5 ingredients and is on the table in less than 30 minutes
What's not to love about that?!
Whether you serve it on the side or as a main dish, it's comfort food that the whole family will love.

  • 8 ounces elbow macaroni
  • 1 ½ cups milk
  • ¼ cup Country Crock® Spread
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1 ½ cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  1. Cook pasta in large pot according to package instructions
  2. Meanwhile, whisk milk, Country Crock® Spread, and flour in large saucepan.
  3. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat, whisking frequently.
  4. Reduce heat and cook, stirring occasionally, until thickened, about 5 minutes.
  5. Stir in cheese until melted; remove from heat. Drain pasta and fold into the sauce.
